Heart Disease Predictor

Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction App. This app uses machine learning to predict the likelihood of heart disease based on various factors. Early detection of heart disease can significantly improve health outcomes. Use this app to assess your risk. This project aims to provide a user-friendly tool for predicting heart disease risk using machine learning. It uses a dataset containing various health metrics to predict the likelihood of heart disease.

Simply fill out the form fields to receive your prediction!
